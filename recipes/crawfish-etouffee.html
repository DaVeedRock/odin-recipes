Crawfish Etoufee Recipe

Description

Crawfish etoufee is a dish from Louisiana typically served over a bed of rice during crawfish season. This particular recipe was curated from the Taste of Home website.

Ingredients

    1/2 cup butter, cubed
    1/2 cup and 2 tablespoons of all purpose flour
    1 1/4 cups chopped celery
