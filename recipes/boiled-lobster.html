Boiled Lobster Recipe

Description

Boiled lobster is a comfort food in my family that is typically served plain with a melted butter dipping sauce as a side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 1-2 pound lobster
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Fill a large pot about halfway full with water. Cover the pot, and bring the water to a boil.
  2. Once the water has reached a boil, place lobster into pot. Cover, set medium heat and let steam for approximately 5 minutes, or until the lobster turns bright red and the tail is firm and opaque when checked.
  3. Remove the lobster from the pot and serve with melted butter.
