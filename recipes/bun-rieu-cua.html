Bun Rieu Cua (Vietnamese Crab Vermicelli Soup) Recipe

Description

Bun rieu cua has its origins in the Southern lowland rice fields of Vietnam, where farmers would harvest paddy crabs from the rice fields. Bun rieu cua is a traditional, flavor-filled classic dish in my family, with this recipe being curated from Hungry Huy's website.
